About

I am a postdoc currently working in collaboration with the Granular Lab at University of Navarra, exploring the control of granular media with vibrations as well as the role of viscosity in sedimentation processes through constrictions.

I completed my Ph.D. in 2021 under the supervision of Laurence Talini and Christian Frétigny at the SIMM lab at ESPCI Paris, applying thermocapillarity in thin liquid films to the study of intermolecular interactions. You can find my Ph.D. thesis here.
